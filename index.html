About

I am in pursuit of making a tangible difference in cancer therapeutics. This motivation comes from my firsthand experience with the drawbacks we face with current treatment options. It frankly baffles me that, in an age of remarkable scientific advances, we continue to be humbled by a disease as prevalent as cancer. And so, I submerge myself in the high seas of cancer chemistry. I want to work towards establishing avenues, especially in countries like my motherland Bangladesh, that make better cancer therapies accessible to the PEOPLE. Importantly, I promise to perpetually fight against ALL forces that aim to disrupt cancer research. My journey has just begun, and I am ready for the next big dive!
